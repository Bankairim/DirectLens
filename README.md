**DirectLens** is an ultra-lightweight, open-source alternative to "Circle to Search" for any Android smartphone running Android 11+. 

Launch Google Lens instantly with a simple long press on your navigation bar, with a premium "Pixel-like" haptic experience.

## ✨ Features
- **Ultra-Lightweight**: Minimal battery consumption, only wakes up when needed.
- **Direct Launch**: No cropping interface, no extra steps. Just press and search.
- **HD Haptic Feedback**: Dynamic "Buzz" experience (1-100% intensity) inspired by Pixel devices.
- **Privacy First**: Zero data collection. Screenshots stay local and go straight to Google Lens.
- **Fully Customizable**: Adjust detection zones, position, and vibration strength.
- **Multilingual**: Supports EN, FR, DE, NL, and ZH.

## ⚙️ Requirements
- Android 11 (API 30) or higher.
- Google App installed (for Google Lens).
- Accessibility Service permission granted.
